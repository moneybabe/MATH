\textbf{Problem 5.}
Let $\Omega = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}$ and the probability is uniformaly distributed. Let $A$ be even numbers and $B$ be odd numbers.
Then $P(A) = \frac{2}{5}$, $P(B) = \frac{3}{5}$, $P(A|B) = \frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)} = 0$, and similarly, $P(B|A) = 0$.
